\# CareerLens Engineering Rules



\## Product Positioning



CareerLens is an AI Career Intelligence SaaS platform.



It is NOT:

\- a resume writer

\- a chatbot



It should provide:

\- structured career intelligence

\- evidence-based analysis

\- explainable recommendations





\## Architecture



Frontend:

\- Vue 3

\- TypeScript

\- Vite



Backend:

\- FastAPI

\- Service layer architecture



AI:

\- Separate ai-service





\## Development Rules



Before modifying code:

1\. Inspect existing architecture.

2\. Reuse existing modules.

3\. Avoid unnecessary refactoring.



After implementation:

1\. Run build/test commands.

2\. Fix all errors.





\## Product Quality



The product should feel like:

\- Notion

\- Linear

\- premium SaaS tools



Avoid:

\- student demo feeling

\- chatbot-only interaction





\## AI Design



Prefer:

\- structured outputs

\- capability mapping

\- evidence

\- confidence scores



Avoid:

\- generic AI summaries
